Demo note: This is a single-file HTML prototype with embedded representative sample data. It is designed from the uploaded notebooks: preprocessing uses daily smart meter consumption, household metadata, daily weather and bank holidays; clustering uses Spark ML K-Means with k=3; forecasting compares Linear Regression, Random Forest, BA-BP NN and GBT with cluster-aware and no-cluster variants. For production, replace the embedded demo arrays with outputs exported from your pipeline, for example daily_forecast.csv, household_clusters.csv, model_metrics.csv, and weather enrichment tables.
